## Terms of Use

We strive to maintain a highly reliable and stable API for the developer community. However, as a free service, it is provided on a **"best effort"** basis. 

While we use robust infrastructure (Google Cloud Run and Zuplo) to ensure high availability, we do not provide strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs) or warranties. We reserve the right to rate-limit or block requests that are abusive or threaten the stability of the service.

## Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and collect only what is necessary to run the service:

- **Personal Data:** Any information you provide when requesting an API key (such as your name, email, or WhatsApp number) is used **exclusively** for provisioning your key, providing support, and contacting you about service updates. We will never sell, share, or use your personal data for marketing purposes.
- **API Payloads:** The text data you send to the API for lemmatization is processed statelessly in memory. We do not store, log, or use your text payloads for training models or any other purpose.
